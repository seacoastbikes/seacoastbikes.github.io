About Us

SABR is an volunteer run organization dedicated to making biking better in the Seacoast. Mission statement. Vision statement.

The work (actually a labor of love!) gets done by dedicated people who share their talents and passion for biking.

We’ve been around since 1992 and helped create significant improvements for local bicycling – be it new infrastructure (e.g. bike paths, bike bridges) and incubating new programs (e.g. VeloKids, Bike to Work Day). Check out our history here.
